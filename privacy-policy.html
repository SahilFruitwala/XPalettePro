XPalettePro Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

XPalettePro is designed to run locally in your browser. This extension does not collect or sell personal information.

What Data Is Collected

XPalettePro does not collect personal data, browsing history, account credentials, messages, contacts, or analytics events.

How Data Is Used

The extension stores only one preference locally in your browser: the selected theme. This is saved under the key xp_theme in chrome.storage.local.

Permissions

  • storage: used only to remember your selected theme.
  • Host permissions for X/Twitter domains: used only to apply styles on those pages.

Network Access

XPalettePro does not send telemetry and does not make external API calls for theming logic. Standard page requests made by X/Twitter still occur as part of normal browsing.

Data Sharing

XPalettePro does not share your data with third parties because it does not collect personal data.

Data Retention

The locally stored theme preference remains in your browser until you change it, clear extension storage, or uninstall the extension.
